Welcome to Holistic Approach

Guiding young minds through coding, math, and chess for a transformative learning experience.

Welcome to my educational hub!

I'm Alex, the creator of this website and your dedicated teacher. Here, young minds immerse themselves in the synergies of coding, math, and chess. At 22, with a background in Electrical and Computer Engineering, I'm passionate about nurturing not only technical proficiency but also meaningful life skills. Join me in guiding your child through a dynamic learning journey that sparks creativity, sharpens logical reasoning, and fosters strategic thinking.

I offer comprehensive courses in coding, math, and chess, tailored to the specific needs and interests of students aged 8-14. My curriculum is designed to take learners from foundational concepts to advanced proficiency, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of each subject.

Explore!

  • Coding

    For coding enthusiasts, we begin with the basics of serial execution, commands, conditionals, loops, functions, and variables.
    Depending on age and experience, we utilize platforms like CodeMonkey, Scratch, or Python to bring concepts to life.

  • Mathematics

    In math, I follow the proven methods of Sal Khan, making use of intuitive approaches
    to ensure students not only grasp concepts but also appreciate the beauty of mathematical thinking.

  • Chess

    In the world of chess, we cover everything from the fundamentals to advanced tactics, strategies, and in-depth analysis.
    My goal is to equip students with a deep understanding of the game, enabling them to compete at a high level.
